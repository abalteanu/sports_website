Here is the Huston Comets Team. They are a team based in Huston, Texas. The team was formed in 1997 and were one of the founding teams of the WNBA. They won the first four Championships of the WNBA's existence. The team was disbanded in 2008 because new ownership could not be found.

The Minnesota Lynx team is based in Minneapolis, Minnesota. They were founded prior to the 1999 season and since, have won the WNBA title four times! The team has qualified for the playoffs for 12 out of 21 years, and has been "home to many high-quality players such as Katie Smith, explosive small forward Seimone Augustus, native Minnesotan Lindsay Whalen, UConn standout Maya Moore, forward Rebekkah Brunson, and center Sylvia Fowles."

Seattle Storm is a team based in Seattle, Washington. Founded in 2000, it has won 4 WNBA titles and has qualified for the playoffs 16 out of 21 years of its existence. The team cultivates a fan-friendly, family environment at home games by having an all-kid dance squad, which leads young fans in a conga line on the court during time-outs, to the music of "C'mon N' Ride It (The Train)" by the Quad City DJ's.
